Sommerholiday

In summer you will find various, beautiful hiking paths with different levels of difficulty and rustic mountain huts, for example the Hofpürglhütte.Our farm is a great start of varied walks where advice is given from Martin who is a certified walking guide, Nordic walking trainer and member of the mountain rescue for many years. Or you may join him on one of the walks with Filzmoos Aktiv.

You can hop on the free hiking bus which takes you conveniently to the start of your walking destination.

Wild mushrooms and fresh blueberries are waiting to be discovered in our forest. In the warm summer evenings you may barbecue on our large playing area where children can spend their time playing table tennis, sliding, swinging or building sand castles.
