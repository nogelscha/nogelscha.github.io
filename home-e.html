Let us tell you about us

Enjoy your holiday in Filzmoos at the foot of the stunning mountain Bischofsmütze.

We, Heidi und Martin Rettenwender, run a small, organic mountain farm in Neuberg/Filzmoos with our family. On our farm you will find cows, calves, horses, cats and a dog.

The hotel Mein Neubergerhof can be reached easily by foot and offers a great menu card.

InSport Stefan you will receive a discount of 15 % on renting ski equipment.

The leisure centre Filzmoos Swimming pool and sauna is free of charge for all our guests.
